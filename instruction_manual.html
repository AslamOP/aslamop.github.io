Instruction Manual

How to Use the Sleeping Mask

  1. Charge the sleeping mask using the provided USB cable until fully charged.
  2. Turn on the Bluetooth feature on your device and pair it with the sleeping mask.
  3. Adjust the mask to fit comfortably over your eyes and ears.
  4. Use the buttons on the mask to control volume, play/pause music, or answer calls.
  5. Activate the heart rate monitor by pressing the designated button.
  6. Enjoy a restful sleep with noise cancellation and gradual music fading.
  7. To clean, gently wipe the mask with a damp cloth and let it air dry.

Troubleshooting

  1. Mask not charging: Ensure the USB cable is securely connected and the power source is functioning.
  2. Bluetooth not pairing: Restart the mask and your device, then try pairing again.
  3. Heart rate monitor not working: Ensure the sensor is clean and properly positioned on your skin.
  4. Music not playing: Check the volume settings and ensure the mask is connected to your device.
