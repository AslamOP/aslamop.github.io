Problem Statement

Existing sleep masks and noise-canceling earbuds are often bulky, rigid, or ill-fitting, causing discomfort, especially for side sleepers. Traditional earbuds can put pressure on the ears, while standard sleep masks may not stay in place throughout the night. There is a need for a lightweight, flexible sleep mask with integrated noise-canceling Bluetooth earbuds and a heart rate monitor that detects sleep onset and gradually lowers music volume, ensuring a comfortable and uninterrupted sleep experience.

Objectives

  • Promote deep & restful sleep with high-quality active noise cancellation and soothing sounds.
  • Ensure user safety with integrated sound recognition to alert users in emergencies.
  • Provide maximum comfort using ultra-soft foam fabric, cooling gel for eyes, and a lightweight design suitable for all sleeping positions.
  • Track sleep patterns and monitor motion sensors to fade music gradually.
  • Offer a feasible, low-cost solution with Bluetooth connectivity, button controls, long-lasting battery life, and fast charging.

Constraints

  • The mask must be lightweight and flexible for comfort in all sleeping positions.
  • Integrated earbuds should be noise-canceling and not exert pressure on the ears.
  • The Bluetooth system should allow seamless connectivity with minimal latency.
  • The music volume must gradually reduce as the user falls asleep.
  • The battery should last through a full night’s sleep.
  • Materials used must be breathable and skin-friendly.
  • The design should ensure a secure yet non-restrictive fit.
  • All components must be compact and unobtrusive.
  • The device should be easy to clean and maintain.

Kano Model

The Kano Model is an advanced version of the sleeping mask, designed with cutting-edge technology to enhance user comfort and functionality. It incorporates the following features:

  • Adaptive noise cancellation that adjusts to ambient sound levels in real-time.
  • Integrated temperature regulation to keep the mask cool and comfortable throughout the night.
  • Customizable fit with adjustable straps and memory foam padding.
  • Enhanced battery life, providing up to 12 hours of continuous use on a single charge.
  • Smart assistant compatibility for hands-free control of music, alarms, and more.
  • Compact and foldable design for easy portability and storage.
