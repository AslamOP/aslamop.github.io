Unit 4: Semiconductor Physics - Formula Sheet

Essential formulas for Semiconductor Physics, including carrier concentration, Hall effect, mobility, and conductivity. Use this as a quick reference for your studies and exams.

Carrier Concentration

ni = sqrt(NcNv) exp(-Eg/2kT)
Nc, Nv: effective density of states, Eg: band gap, k: Boltzmann constant, T: temperature
n ≈ ND (n-type, fully ionized)
p ≈ NA (p-type, fully ionized)

Hall Effect

RH = Ey / (JxBz) = 1/(nq)
Ey: Hall field, Jx: current density, Bz: magnetic field, n: carrier concentration, q: charge

Mobility & Conductivity

μ = vd / E
vd: drift velocity, E: electric field
σ = nqμ
