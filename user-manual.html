Introduction

Thank you for purchasing the Coffee Alarm Clock! This product is designed to wake you up with an instant cup of coffee, tea, or any other instant beverage. We present an easy-to-use alarm clock integrated with a drip-based coffee maker.

Key Features

  • Automated brewing at pre-set alarm time
  • Supports coffee, tea, and other instant drinks
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Energy-efficient heating system
  • Easy-to-clean detachable parts

Safety Instructions

  • Do not immerse the unit in water
  • Keep away from children and pets
  • Always unplug after use
  • Use clean water and avoid overfilling
  • Place on a stable, flat surface

Installation

  1. Unbox and check parts
  2. Place on a rigid surface
  3. Fill tank, attach filter, plug in

Operating Instructions

  1. Set alarm using buttons and confirm
  2. Add beverage powder and fill tank
  3. Choose brew strength (if applicable)
  4. Alarm will trigger automatic brewing
