Introduction

Thank you for purchasing the Coffee Alarm Clock! This product is designed to wake you up with an instant cup of coffee, tea, or any other instant beverage.

Key Features

  • Automated brewing at pre-set alarm time
  • Supports coffee, tea, and other instant drinks
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Energy-efficient heating system
  • Easy-to-clean detachable parts

Physical Specifications

Dimensions: 25 cm (L) × 20 cm (W) × 20 cm (H)

Safety Instructions

  • Do not immerse the main unit in water
  • Keep away from children and pets
  • Always unplug after use
  • Use only clean, fresh water
  • Do not use metal/sharp objects inside the unit
  • Ensure stable placement on a flat, dry surface

Installation

  1. Unbox and remove packaging
  2. Place on a flat surface and plug into outlet
  3. Fill water tank with clean water
  4. Insert coffee or tea into filter basket

Operating Instructions

  1. Set alarm time using the buttons
  2. Press confirm to save
  3. Ensure water reservoir is full
  4. When alarm rings, brewing starts automatically
