Important Formulas

Unit 1: Quantum Mechanics

E = hν (Energy of a photon)

λ = h/p (de Broglie wavelength)

Δx·Δp ≥ ħ/2 (Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle)

Unit 2: Laser & Fiber Optics

Population Inversion: N₂ > N₁

Critical Angle: θc = sin⁻¹(n₂/n₁)

Numerical Aperture: NA = √(n₁² - n₂²)

Unit 3: Dielectric & Magnetic Materials

Polarization: P = χeE

Magnetic Susceptibility: χm = M/H

Capacitance: C = ε₀εrA/d

Unit 4: Semiconductor Physics

Conductivity: σ = nqμ

Energy Gap: Eg = Ec - Ev

Drift Current: J = σE
